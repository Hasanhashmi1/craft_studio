Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 28, 2023

Important: At Craft Studio, we value your privacy. We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information. Our mission is solely to create entertaining YouTube content for our audience.

Introduction

Welcome to Craft Studio's Privacy Policy. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our website and the choices you have associated with that data.

We operate the Craft Studio website and the associated YouTube channel "Rockstar Hannan" which features content created for entertainment purposes.

Information Collection and Use

We want to be perfectly clear: we do not collect any personal information from visitors to our website or viewers of our YouTube content.

Unlike many websites and online services, we have intentionally designed our platform to function without gathering personal data. This means:

  • We don't use tracking cookies
  • We don't have user accounts or registration systems
  • We don't require email addresses or any contact information
  • We don't use analytics tools that track individual users
  • We don't store any information about your visit to our website

Third-Party Services

Our website embeds YouTube videos using iframes. When you watch these videos, YouTube may collect information as described in their Privacy Policy. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy of YouTube ( operated by Google) if you have concerns about their data practices:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Protection

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no risk of your information being compromised through our website. We've adopted a "no data collection" approach as the most effective way to protect our audience's privacy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Describe how the target audience receives the goods or services promoted in your ads.

Craft Studio does not sell any products or services directly. Any promotional content we create is exclusively for entertainment purposes on YouTube. We do not engage in e-commerce or direct sales of any kind. Our content is freely accessible to our audience without any requirements for personal information or payment.

2. Please explain how the products / services are provided:

We provide no products or services for sale. Our only offering is entertainment content through YouTube videos. This content is freely available to view on the YouTube platform without any registration requirements or fees. We are solely content creators, not product or service providers.

3. Please describe how your company interacts with your target audience.

Our interaction with our audience is limited to:

  • Publishing entertaining video content on YouTube
  • Responding to comments on our YouTube videos (when appropriate)
  • Occasionally engaging with our audience through YouTube Community features
We do not collect contact information, maintain mailing lists, or conduct direct marketing campaigns. All interactions occur within the YouTube platform according to their terms of service and privacy policies.

4. Who are your customers / target audience? (Please describe both, if they're different.)

We do not have "customers" in the traditional sense as we do not sell any products or services. Our target audience consists of viewers who enjoy our YouTube content. This typically includes:

  • YouTube users interested in entertainment content
  • Individuals looking for creative and engaging video content
  • Viewers who appreciate the specific style of content we produce
Since we do not collect demographic data, we cannot provide specific details about our audience composition. Our content is intended for general audiences who choose to watch our videos on YouTube.
